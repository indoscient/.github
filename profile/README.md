### 💡 Our Solution
We enable researchers, banks, insurers, and data owners to monetize or co-develop insights **without ever exposing raw records**.

#### 🔒 Core Technologies
- **End‑to‑End Encryption**: Data is encrypted at rest & in transit; never leaves the owner’s vault.
- **Federated Learning Engine**: Buyers train ML models directly on encrypted shards; only model weights are exchanged.
- **Differential‑Privacy Guarantees**: Every query consumes a privacy budget, ensuring mathematically proven anonymity.
- **Smart‑Contract Escrow**: Payments released only after verifiable model‑performance thresholds are met.
- **Regulatory Dashboard**: Real‑time audit logs, consent receipts, and export‑ready compliance reports.
